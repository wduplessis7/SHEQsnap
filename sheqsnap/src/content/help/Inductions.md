# Inductions & Training

## What This Module Tracks

The Inductions module allows you to record and manage safety inductions for both employees and contractors. It tracks who was inducted, what type of induction was completed, when it was conducted, and when it expires.

## When to Log an Induction

Record an induction whenever:
- A new employee joins the organisation and completes their site safety induction
- A contractor arrives on site and is inducted before starting work
- An employee or contractor completes a specialised induction (e.g., PPE, Chemical Handling, Equipment Operation)
- A legal appointment induction is completed
- An existing induction is renewed after expiry

## Induction Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Site Safety Induction | General site safety rules, emergency procedures, and hazard awareness |
| Fire & Emergency Procedures | Fire evacuation routes, assembly points, and emergency response |
| PPE Requirements | Correct use, maintenance, and inspection of personal protective equipment |
| Chemical Handling & HazCom | Safe handling of hazardous chemicals and substances |
| Equipment / Plant Operation | Safe operation of specific machinery or plant equipment |
| Environmental Awareness | Environmental responsibilities, waste management, and spill procedures |
| Legal Appointment | Formal induction for legally appointed roles (e.g., Section 16.2, 8.1) |
| Other | Any other induction type not covered above |

## Employee vs Contractor Records

- **Employee** inductions are for permanent or fixed-term staff members
- **Contractor** inductions are for third-party contractors, subcontractors, and temporary workers

Filtering by type allows you to quickly view all contractor inductions on site, which is particularly useful for compliance audits.

## Validity Periods

When recording an induction, you can set a validity period:
- **No expiry** — for once-off inductions that do not need renewal
- **3, 6, 12, 24, or 36 months** — the system automatically calculates the expiry date from the conducted date
- **Custom date** — set a specific expiry date

The system will automatically change the status of an induction as it approaches or passes its expiry date:
- **Current** — valid and not due for renewal
- **Expiring Soon** — expires within the next 30 days
- **Expired** — past the expiry date and requires renewal

## Expiry Alerts

The stats panel at the top of the Inductions list shows counts for Current, Expiring Soon, and Expired inductions. Rows are colour-coded: amber for expiring soon, red for expired.
