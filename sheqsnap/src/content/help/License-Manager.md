# License Manager

---

## What is the License Manager?

The License Manager is a centralised register for tracking all compliance documents held by employees and contractors on site. It is designed to ensure that no one operates without the required qualifications and that expiring documents are caught and renewed before they lapse.

---

## What to Track in the License Manager

The License Manager can be used to track any document that has an expiry date and must be renewed to maintain compliance. This includes:

| Document Type | Examples |
|--------------|---------|
| **Operator Licences** | Driver's licence, forklift operator, crane operator, scaffolding |
| **Safety Certifications** | First Aid, Working at Heights, Fire Safety, Hazmat Handling |
| **Trade Certificates** | Electrical certificate, boilermaker, welding, rigging |
| **Legal Appointments** | Section 16(2) appointments, Section 8 designations, Safety Representative appointments under the OHSA or MHSA |
| **Permits** | Environmental permits, pressure vessel inspection certificates, any statutory permit required by legislation |
| **Other Compliance Documents** | Any certificate, permit, or authorisation that has an expiry date and must be renewed |

---

## How Expiry Notifications Work

SHEQsnap monitors the expiry dates of all tracked documents and automatically flags records based on proximity to expiry:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | The document is current and not due for renewal |
| **Expiring Soon** | The document expires within the next 30 days — action is required |
| **Expired** | The document has lapsed — the holder should not be permitted to perform regulated tasks until renewed |

A badge count on the **Licenses** sidebar link shows the total number of documents that are either expiring soon or already expired, so you can respond quickly without having to open the module first.

---

## How to Add a License

1. Click **Licenses** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add License**
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Holder Name** — the employee or contractor who holds the document
   - **Holder Type** — Employee or Contractor
   - **License / Document Type** — select from the list or choose "Other"
   - **License Number** — the certificate or permit number (optional but recommended)
   - **Issue Date** — when the document was issued
   - **Expiry Date** — when the document expires (required)
   - **Notes** — any additional context (optional)
4. Click **Save**

---

## Tips

- Set up a routine (e.g. weekly) to check the Licenses list for anything marked **Expiring Soon**
- Use the sidebar badge count as a quick indicator — if it shows a number, action is needed
- Track legal appointments (e.g. Section 16(2) designations) alongside operator licences to maintain a complete compliance picture
- Expired documents should be renewed before the holder performs any regulated work
